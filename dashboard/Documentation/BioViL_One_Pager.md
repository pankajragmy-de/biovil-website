# BioViL Energy KPI Dashboard — Executive Summary (One-Pager) 

## The Vision
The **BioViL Energy KPI Dashboard** is a state-of-the-art tracking and analytics tool designed for rural bioenergy deployments. By moving beyond static reporting, the dashboard uses real-time, dynamic algorithmic tracking to calculate project impacts instantly.

## Live Deployment: Prototype #1 (Rampur, Assam)
*   **System Specifications:** 4 m³ Underground Fixed-Dome Biodigester.
*   **Operational Since:** January 15, 2026.
*   **Feedstock Strategy:** **100% Animal Manure** (diverting roughly 600 kg of rotting cow dung from open ponds each month).

## The Three Pillars of Impact

### 1. Environmental Stewardship
Based on conservative IPCC Tier-1 estimates and local Assam calculations:
*   The diversion of 600 kg of wet slurry prevents the release of high-potency uncaptured methane, avoiding **218 kg CO₂e/month**.
*   The biogas output directly displaces indoor wood-burning stoves, avoiding the burning of 120 kg of firewood and saving an additional **220.1 kg CO₂e/month**.
*   Total Baseline Averted: **438.1 kg CO₂e per month.**

### 2. Social Capacity & Independence
BioViL is committed to rural independence and capacity building.
*   **Health:** Delivering **3 hours/day** of smoke-free indoor air to the household, dramatically lowering particulate matter (PM) exposure.
*   **Employment:** Successfully hired **5 Local Experts** through the initial Catalyst Phase budget, injecting capital directly back into the community.
*   **Education:** Exceeded initial targets by training **4 Community Operators** to manage, maintain, and own the biodigester infrastructure.

### 3. Transparent Financial Deployment
The dashboard provides a complete, clear accounting of capital deployment.
*   **Funding Profile:** €3,000 Catalyst Grant + €600 Private Equity.
*   **Utilization Engine:** 100% of the €3,000 grant was allocated to local infrastructure (€1,559), IT/Website (€952), local labor (€303), and transport ops (€115). No funds were lost to excessive administrative overhead.

## Advanced Biological Modeling
The dashboard is powered by a JavaScript algorithmic engine that automatically adjusts biogas output (m³) based on **Assam’s mesophilic temperature curve**. It accurately reflects the natural winter-time dip in biodigester efficiency and the robust summer-time accelerations, providing stakeholders with a mathematically sound, continuously updating timeline of returns.
