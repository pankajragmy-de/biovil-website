Who Are You?

Find Your Path Into BioViL.

🌾

I'm a Farmer

Do you have agricultural land with crop waste or animal dung? You may be eligible to host a BioViL biodigester, earning clean fuel and additional income from waste you'd otherwise discard.

🤝

I'm an Organisation

Looking to bring clean energy to the communities you serve? Partner with BioViL to co-implement, co-fund, or co-research waste-to-energy solutions at the village level.

💹

I'm an Investor

BioViL's Farm-to-Business model offers multiple revenue channels, biogas sales, carbon credits, organic fertilizer, alongside measurable social and environmental returns.
