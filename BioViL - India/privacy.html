Privacy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

BioViL's website is an informational site. We keep data collection to the minimum a static website can have: no cookies, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking scripts.

What we receive

  • Contact form: when you submit the form on our contact page, we receive the details you type (name, email, and your message) and use them only to reply to you. They are not shared with anyone else or used for marketing.
  • Email: if you email us directly, your message is handled the same way.

Website chatbot

The chat widget on our homepage runs entirely in your browser. Your messages are not sent to or stored on any server.

Technical service providers

Like most websites, some infrastructure providers technically receive your IP address when pages and their resources load:

  • GitHub Pages hosts this website and serves every page.
  • Unsplash and Wikimedia serve some photographs.
  • Cloudflare (cdnjs), jsDelivr and unpkg serve open-source display libraries on some pages.

Fonts are hosted on our own domain — no font provider receives your data. We do not pass any personal information to any of the providers above; they see only the standard technical data involved in serving a web page.

Your rights

If you have contacted us and would like your correspondence deleted, or have any question about your data, email contact@biovil.co.in and we will take care of it.
